A whole team of us are working this summer on Haskell tooling projects in order to take Haskell tooling to the next level. Starting from the solid foundations of ghcide we are building haskell-language-server which will suceed haskell-ide-engine as the IDE of choice for Haskell developers.

We are focused on building a robust IDE experience which works on any Haskell project, built with any build system and of any size.
